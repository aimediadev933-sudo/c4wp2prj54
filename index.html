Explore Our Training

About Our Program

This project addressed the urgent need for enhanced cybersecurity capabilities within the National Research and Education Networks (NRENs) of seven least developed Asian countries. Through a series of three online hands-on workshops, we equipped 96 IT professionals with critical knowledge and skills to identify, mitigate, and manage web and application security threats.

Led by the University of Dhaka, the initiative aimed to build resilient and secure digital infrastructures in academic institutions, while also promoting regional collaboration and gender inclusion in the cybersecurity domain.

Our Mission

To enhance web and application security capacity among NREN professionals in LDC countries through hands-on training, building technical expertise and promoting inclusive participation in cybersecurity.

Gender Inclusion Initiative

Women Empowerment in Cybersecurity

Our program prioritized gender inclusion with dedicated efforts to engage female professionals in cybersecurity training. Despite challenges in some regions, we successfully trained 31 women, including a dedicated women-focused session.

This initiative represents a significant step toward building gender-diverse cybersecurity capacity in the NREN community across least developed countries.
